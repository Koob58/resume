Philosophy

I love simplicity, minimalism, and clarity, as exemplified in this resume. In Rails I prefer following REST principles, but I never stay blindly hooked on paradigms. There are always exceptions. I love clean code and refactoring (and I read both of these books). I've been extensively exploring architecture and design patterns, and over the years realized that coming up with simple architecture is much harder (and more rewarding) than with a complex one.
